Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the minimum age to rent a vehicle?

The minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21 years old with a valid driver's license.

2. Do I need to provide a security deposit?

Yes, a refundable security deposit is required for all rentals.

3. What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

You need a valid driver's license and an ID proof such as a passport or national ID card.

4. Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period by contacting our customer service.

5. What is the fuel policy?

The vehicle should be returned with the same amount of fuel as when it was picked up.

6. Are there any mileage limits?

Mileage limits may apply depending on the vehicle and rental duration. Please check the terms before booking.

7. Can I rent a vehicle for a one-way trip?

Yes, one-way rentals are available, but additional fees may apply. Please check with our customer service.

8. What happens if I return the vehicle late?

Late returns may incur additional charges. Please inform us as soon as possible if you expect to return the vehicle later than planned.

9. Is insurance included in the rental cost?

Basic insurance coverage is included, but you may opt for additional coverage for extra protection.

10. Can I add an additional driver?

Yes, you can add an additional driver for a nominal fee. Both drivers must be present at the time of rental and provide valid licenses.
